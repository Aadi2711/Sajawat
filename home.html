Meet Our Founder

Bhumika Dhawan, Founder Designer

Bhumika Dhawan, the founder and CEO of Interiors by Sajawat By Bhumika, is a Delhi University graduate with a degree in interior design. Her initial dream of becoming a fashion designer shifted to interior design due to her passion for drawing and sketching.

With Bhumika's vision and a dedicated team, their mission is to turn their clients' dreams into reality by creating personalized, functional, and aesthetically pleasing living spaces. Their goal is to transform homes into luxurious and comfortable environments that mirror the client's personality.

Sajawat By Bhumika's motto is centered around creating functional and visually appealing spaces that cater to their clients' specific needs. They emphasize sustainable and universal design principles and take special care to understand each client's preferences and requirements.

Located in Delhi, Sajawat by Bhumika specializes in residential interior development and turnkey project management. They offer a wide range of services, from Full Home Interior Design to Execution, effectively bringing their clients' visions to life.
